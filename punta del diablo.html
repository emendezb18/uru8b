PUNTA DEL DIABLO

what is the punta del diablo

Punta del Diablo is a small fishing town and seaside resort on the east coast of Uruguay. The Playa de los Pescadores in the center is lined with fishing boats and features the white monument De Artigas a Bolívar on its rocks. Nearby are stalls selling handicrafts and Rivero Beach, known for its strong waves.

Information about punta del diablo

Currently (2009) it has been integrated into the urban and tourist development of Punta del Este. On the west side there is a small natural port, where water sports are developed, and the Portezuelo and Solanas beaches, much visited for their orientation to the west, their calm waters and their large expanses of fine sand. On the east side there are caves, rocks and coarse sand beaches, with views of Punta del Este and Gorriti Island. In the same way, on the east side you can visit the famous Casapueblo House-Workshop, designed by the plastic artist Carlos Páez Vilaró.
