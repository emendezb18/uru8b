what is the playa mansa?

Playa Mansa is for all ages, ideal for children and for those who want to take a pleasant swim in the sea, where there are few waves, usually small in size, the beach stretches from west to east, from stop 24 in the Las Delicias area to stop 1 of Punta del Este. The coast of La Mansa covers well-known Punta Este neighborhoods such as California Park, Cantegril, Marconi or Pine Beach
