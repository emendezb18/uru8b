Information about valle de lunarejo

It is one of the protected areas of Uruguay, since 2009 it has been part of the National System of Protected Areas (SNAP). It is made up of a valley criss-crossed by various streams, hills with heights over 350 meters, numerous waterfalls and waterfalls, as well as forests and pastures. The area has diverse natural environments, where there is a wide diversity in terms of fauna and flora
