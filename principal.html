Uruguay Climate

The climate is temperate and humid (average 17 °C), with hot summers and more or less homogeneous rainfall throughout the year. Due to its latitude, between 30ºS and 35ºS, the four seasons are clearly differentiated.

Weather

For example, the coldest month in Uruguay is July and the hottest is January.
